🏥 MedIntel AI – Hospital Management Analytics

📌 Project Overview

MedIntel AI is an end-to-end Hospital Management Analytics project developed to analyze hospital operations and generate business insights using PostgreSQL, SQL, Python, and Power BI.

The project simulates a real hospital environment by managing patient records, doctors, admissions, billing, pharmacy, emergency visits, insurance claims, and more.
